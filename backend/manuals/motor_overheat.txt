EcoStream Dispatch – Service Manual Excerpt: Motor Overheat\n\nSymptoms\n- Motor temperature rises above 85°C for sustained periods\n- Power derating, reduced acceleration, possible inverter warnings\n\nImmediate actions\n1) Reduce speed and load; avoid aggressive acceleration\n2) Check coolant pump operation and coolant level\n3) Inspect radiator fins for blockage (dust, plastic, mud)\n4) Verify fan engagement and airflow direction\n\nRoot-cause checklist\n- Coolant leak or low coolant\n- Pump failure or intermittent relay\n- Fan controller fault\n- Thermal paste degradation at motor/inverter interface\n\nWhen to stop\n- If temperature exceeds 95°C or warning persists > 3 minutes: pull over safely\n
